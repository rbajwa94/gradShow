Alyssa Willis

Designer

My name is Alyssa Willis. I am from Campbell River and I am in the Communication Design program. Before North Island College, I was a certified Nail Technician after studying at Del Rio Academy. In my program, I enjoyed learning all the facets of design, from the principles of design to colour, typography and grids. I appreciated the push that the critiques gave me to explore new ideas and try new things. The biggest thing I have learned from the program is to be confident in my design choices and how to look at things differently. After graduation I plan to work in the print and web design field. I will also continue creating art strictly for the passion of creating.

True Blue Pet Supplies

The project I am displaying in the showcase is a rebranding project that was done in the Communication Design 2 course. We were asked to choose a local business and give them a fresh new look from logo and stationery to packaging and collateral branding. I chose a local business called True Blue Pet Supplies because I care very much about animals and I admire that they carry organic, all natural pet supplies.
